Earning Stars

Stars cannot be earned on purchases of alcohol, Starbucks Cards or Starbucks Card reloads.

  • Earn 1 Star per $1 spent when you scan your member barcode in the app, then pay with cash, credit/debit cards or mobile wallets at participating stores.
  • You can also earn 1 Star per $1 spent when you link a payment method and pay directly through the app.
  • Earn 2 Stars per $1 spent when you load funds and pay with your digital Starbucks Card in the app.
  • You can also earn 2 Stars per $1 spent when you pay in-person at a participating store with your registered physical Starbucks Card.

Benefits

Free refills available during same in-store visit only. To qualify for the Birthday Reward, you must have made at least one Star-earning transaction.

Terms of Use

For full program details visit starbucks.com/rewards/terms.

Starbucks® Rewards benefits are available at participating Starbucks stores. Visit the Starbucks Store Locator and search for locations honoring “Redeem Rewards”.

Redeeming Rewards

Rewards cannot be redeemed for alcoholic beverages or multi-serve items. You may pay the difference for any beverage customization over $1 and/or merchandise item over $20. Not all stores honor tiered Rewards. Select stores redeem 200 Stars for free food or drink items only.

Notes:

  1. Excludes taxes and gratuities. Some restrictions apply. Flights purchased close to departure may not earn double Stars. Stars and miles may not be earned on alcohol, Starbucks Cards and reloads. More info
  2. Linked Card members will earn 2% Cash Back at participating stores. Stars may not be earned on alcohol or unregistered cards. Terms
  3. Marriott Bonvoy members can earn bonus points or stars for qualifying purchases at select hotels. More details
